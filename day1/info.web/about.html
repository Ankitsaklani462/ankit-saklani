Toyota Boshoku Device India is a wholly owned subsidiary of Toyota Boshoku Asia, specializing in automotive interior systems and components. Company Overview Toyota Boshoku Device India Private Limited (CIN: U35122HR2014PTC056763) is a private limited company incorporated on December 15, 2014, and is currently active. Its registered office is located at Unit 108, Suncity Success Tower, Sector 65, Golf Course Extension Road, Gurugram, Haryana, India, 122002. The company operates primarily in the manufacturing sector, focusing on automotive interior systems and components, including seat slides, seat recliners, and other interior products. It also engages in the production of filters and powertrain components and fabric goods for automotive applications. Toyota Boshoku Corporation +3 Ownership and Corporate Structure As of March 24, 2025, Toyota Boshoku Asia Co., Ltd. acquired the remaining 20% stake in TBDI from Aisin Shiroki, making TBDI a wholly owned subsidiary of Toyota Boshoku Asia. This followed the transfer of non-seat products from TBDI to Aisin's Indian operations. TBDI is part of the Toyota Boshoku Group, which is a global supplier of automotive interior systems and filters. Toyota Boshoku Corporation +1 Financials For the financial year ending March 31, 2025, TBDI reported a revenue of ₹604 crore, with a compounded annual growth rate (CAGR) of 7% over the previous year. The company’s authorized share capital is ₹186,500,000, and the paid-up capital is ₹462,900. The majority of its turnover comes from manufacturing operations.
