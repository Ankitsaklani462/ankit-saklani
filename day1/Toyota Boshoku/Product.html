Our Products

Toyota Boshoku Device India Private Limited specializes in manufacturing high-quality automotive seating components designed for safety, comfort, and performance. As part of the global Toyota Boshoku Corporation group, we ensure world-class engineering standards in every product.

🔧 Seat Slider


A precision-engineered component that allows smooth forward and backward adjustment of car seats. Built for durability, safety, and ease of use, ensuring passenger comfort and driving convenience.

🔩 Seat Recliner


Designed to adjust the seat back angle, providing ergonomic support and improved seating posture. Manufactured with high-strength materials for long-lasting performance and reliability.

🚗 Automotive Seat Mechanisms


Integrated systems combining sliders and recliners to deliver complete seating solutions for modern vehicles. These systems are tested for strength, safety, and durability under global standards.
