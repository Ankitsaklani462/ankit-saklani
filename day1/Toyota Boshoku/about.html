Toyota Boshoku Device India Private Limited is a leading automotive components manufacturer and a proud part of the global Toyota Boshoku Corporation group. Established in 2014, the company operates a modern manufacturing facility in IMT Bawal, Haryana, India. We specialize in the design and manufacturing of high-quality automotive seating components such as seat sliders and seat recliners. Our products are engineered with precision to ensure safety, comfort, and performance in modern vehicles. With a strong focus on quality, innovation, and continuous improvement, we follow global manufacturing standards and lean production practices. Our commitment is to deliver reliable solutions that meet the expectations of leading automobile manufacturers.
